About me

Who am I?

Nothing special. Just a regular accounting major that learns to code, makes ads and writes about psychology and many other subjects. But I'm a paradox, my day job is that of a commerce student but nothing in my life has anything to do with it. It's better to expect my scattered minded, unpredictable behaviour so not to disappoint yourself :)

Why this blog?

I don't neccesarily want to be a writer but I have indeed tried a few times to write and earn some bucks with it. No luck so far. I used to write on substack, blogger and medium, all of which are either very tough to monetize or pay very less.

So i thought, if i have to write for free, why not write on my own website? My reason for making this website is to use it as a personal diary. It's less of a blog and more of documentation of the things I do and learn.

What do I write about?

Whatever piques my interest at that moment. I have tried committing to a single niche many times but it just feels miserable. I intend to write about anything and everything that i want to write about.
